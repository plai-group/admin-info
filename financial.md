## Student Fees
The lab aims to cover [student fees](https://www.grad.ubc.ca/prospective-students/tuition-fees-cost-living/graduate-student-fees) incured by students. 

At the start of every semester, students need to fill [this form](https://www.grad.ubc.ca/sites/default/files/forms/awards_recommendation_form_aug_2019.xlsx) and submit it to barney.
